Diplomat Hotel

A historic hotel with stunning views

About Diplomat Hotel

The Diplomat Hotel is a historic hotel located on top of Dominican Hill in Baguio City. Originally built in the 1910s, it has served various purposes over the years, including a retreat for priests and a hotel for tourists.

The hotel is known for its unique architecture and panoramic views of the city and surrounding mountains. It has become a popular destination for both tourists and locals, offering a glimpse into Baguio's rich history.

Today, the Diplomat Hotel stands as a testament to the city's heritage, attracting visitors who wish to explore its historical significance and enjoy the breathtaking scenery.

How to Get There

From Baguio City Center:

The Diplomat Hotel is located a short drive from the city center, making it easily accessible by taxi or private vehicle.

Driving Directions:
  1. Start at Session Road
  2. Head towards the direction of the Baguio Cathedral
  3. Follow the signs to Dominican Hill
  4. The hotel will be on your left

From Other Parts of Baguio:

Jeepneys and taxis are available throughout the city with "Diplomat Hotel" as a common destination.

By Public Transportation:
  • Look for jeepneys with "Mansion" or "Dominican Hill" signage
