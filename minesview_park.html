Mines View Park

Breathtaking views of abandoned mines and mountains

About Mines View Park

Mines View Park is one of the most scenic spots in Baguio, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the abandoned gold and copper mines of Itogon, Benguet, and the surrounding mountain ranges. The park is perched atop a ridge northeast of Baguio City, about 10 kilometers from the city center.

Originally called "Mines View Park" because of its overlooking view of the mining town of Itogon, the park has become one of Baguio's most popular tourist destinations. Visitors flock to the observation deck to take in the spectacular scenery and cool mountain air.

The park is also known for its souvenir shops selling locally made products, silver jewelry, wood crafts, and the chance to dress up in traditional Igorot costumes for memorable photos.

Park Features

  • Observation deck with panoramic mountain views
  • Traditional Igorot costume rentals for photos
  • Various souvenir and handicraft shops
  • St. Bernard's dog photo opportunities
  • Nearby attractions: The Mansion and Wright Park
  • Local strawberry and peanut vendors

Activities

Traditional Costume Photos

Dress up in colorful Igorot costumes and take memorable photos with the scenic backdrop.

Handicraft Shopping

Browse through unique silver jewelry, wood carvings, and other local handicrafts.

St. Bernard Dog Photos

Take photos with the famous St. Bernard dogs wearing Igorot costumes.

Try Local Delicacies

Sample fresh strawberries, strawberry taho, and other Baguio specialties.

Visitor Information

Location

Northeast of Baguio City, near The Mansion and Wright Park

Operating Hours

Daily, 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Entrance Fee

Free admission

Activity Fees

  • Igorot costume rental: ₱50-100 per set
  • St. Bernard dog photos: ₱50-100
  • Horseback riding nearby: ₱150-300 per round
Get Directions

How to Get There

From Baguio City Center:

Mines View Park is about 3.5 kilometers from downtown Baguio. The most convenient way is by taxi or jeepney.

By Taxi:
  • Simply tell the driver "Mines View Park"
  • Estimated fare: ₱100-150 from downtown
  • Travel time: 10-15 minutes depending on traffic

By Public Transportation:

Several jeepney routes pass by or near Mines View Park.

Jeepney Directions:
  1. Look for jeepneys with "Mines View" signage
  2. Tell the driver when to drop you off
  3. Walk about 200 meters uphill to reach the park
  4. Fare is about ₱10-15 per person
